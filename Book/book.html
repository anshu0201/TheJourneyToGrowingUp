AUTHOR'S NOTE

As a psychaitrist I frequently have a chance to examine strife in a objective way. 
This strife often comes packaged in unexpected experiences. Later in life,with some 
imaginative enhancement, these unexpected experiences transform into
anecdotes which afre related to friends as a means of celebrating new beginnings.

This collection of short stories is an attempt to depict this strife through such
 anecdotes. All, but on of these tangled tales,are based on true life incidents. I will 
 leave it to the readers to guess which one isn't.
I have attempted to craft one story titled "Comfort" in the genre of a 55-word story.
                                                                   NJ 
								Gangtok, 13th June 2015
