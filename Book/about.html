ABOUT THE BOOK

This collection of short stories written by a psychaitrist brings 
to life a range of emotions and explores everyday conflicts
across the lifespan.The tales based on true incidents are thought
provoking and comic at the same time. The many heart arming
plots include :

* A five year-old boy estimates the true value of father's treasure...*
* An adolescent undertakes a journey alone and discovers how adult thinks...*
* A faith-healer pays the ultimate price for his antics...*
* Mental cobwebs are cleared this diwali...*


And other tales that will surely touch your heart....
